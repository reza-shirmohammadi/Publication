Techno-economic assessment and optimization of a solar-assisted industrialpost-combustion CO2 capture and utilization plant.

Reza Shirmohammadi, Alireza Aslani, Roghayeh Ghasempoura,LuisM.Romeo,  Fontina Petrakopoulou.

Energy Reports. 2021

Techno-economic assessment and SAM-based optimization of a solar-assisted industrial post-combustion CO2 capture and utilization plant, validated for Almeria with 0.7 solar share and 18h thermal storage.
