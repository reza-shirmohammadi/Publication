# Publication
Techno-economic assessment and optimization of a solar-assisted industrialpost-combustion CO2 capture and utilization plant.
Reza Shirmohammadi, Alireza Aslani, Roghayeh Ghasempoura,LuisM.Romeo,  Fontina Petrakopoulou.
Energy Reports. 2021
As lead modeler using SAM, I optimized a solar-assisted industrial CO2 recovery unit. My con-figuration achieved a 0.7 solar share, 18h storage, and 3.85 ¢/kWh LCOH. Validation in Almeria proved its global scalability for drastic carbon footprint reduction.
